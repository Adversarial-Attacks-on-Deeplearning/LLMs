Quick Facts

  • Capital: Paris
  • Population: ~67M
  • Language: French
  • Currency: Euro (€)

History

From Gaulish tribes through Roman conquest to modern republic—France’s history is rich and complex.

Explore the medieval castles of the Loire Valley, the revolution of 1789, and the world wars.

Culture

France has given the world fine art, philosophy, fashion, and cinema.

Visit the Louvre, stroll the streets of Montmartre, or relax in a sidewalk café.

Travel Tips

  1. Learn a few basic French phrases.
  2. Validate your metro ticket.
  3. Beware of pickpockets in crowded areas.
  4. Try local boulangeries early in the morning.

Cuisine

From baguettes and cheese to coq au vin and crème brûlée—French food is a delight.

Don’t miss regional specialties like bouillabaisse in Marseille or tarte flambée in Alsace.
