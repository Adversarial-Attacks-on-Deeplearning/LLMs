Your Guide to Brewing Coffee

Tip: Use fresh, cold water filtered for best taste.

Step 1: Grind

Grind your beans to a medium-coarse consistency for pourover.

Step 2: Heat Water

Bring water to the proper temperature (typically between 90–96 °C).

Step 3: Brew

  1. Bloom grounds with a small pour.
  2. Continue pouring in circles.
  3. Let drip for 3–4 minutes.

Enjoy your perfect cup!
